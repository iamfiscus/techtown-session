# Brainstorm Converge

Using the brainstorm-diverge-converge framework, take the idea list below and run the full workflow.

**Problem context:**
Our support team handles 1,400 tickets per week with a 22-minute average handle time. We have 6 agents, each costing $65K/year. We need to reduce ticket volume and handle time while maintaining (or improving) customer satisfaction.

**Phase 1 — Diverge:**
Starting from the idea list I provide, expand to at least 20 ideas. Add your own ideas that build on, combine, or extend the originals. No judgment yet — quantity over quality.

**Phase 2 — Cluster:**
Group all ideas into 4-8 natural themes. Name each cluster clearly.

**Phase 3 — Converge:**
Score each cluster on three criteria using a 1-10 scale:
- **Impact** — How much could this reduce ticket volume or handle time?
- **Feasibility** — How realistic is this to implement with a small team in 90 days?
- **Speed-to-value** — How quickly would we see measurable results?

Calculate a composite score (simple average of the three). Select the **top 3 ideas or clusters** and explain why they won.
