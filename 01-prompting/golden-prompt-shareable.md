# The Golden Prompt
## Screenshot This

---

```
I want you to interview me one question at a time
about [WHAT YOU'RE BUILDING].

Give me an example of a response to make me think
and explain the technical terms in a non-technical way.
```

---

## How to Use It

1. **Open ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini** (free)

2. **Paste the prompt** and replace [WHAT YOU'RE BUILDING]:
   - "...about adding a partner invite feature to my pregnancy app"
   - "...about building a booking system for my salon"
   - "...about creating a dashboard for my customers"

3. **Answer the AI's questions** one at a time

4. **When done, ask:**
   ```
   Based on our conversation, create a specification
   document I can use to build this feature.
   ```

5. **Take that spec to Lovable** (or your building tool)

---

## Why This Works

| Part of the Prompt | What It Does |
|--------------------|--------------|
| "interview me" | AI asks YOU (you don't need to know what to ask) |
| "one question at a time" | Prevents overwhelm |
| "example of a response" | Shows you what good looks like |
| "explain technical terms" | You learn as you go |
| "non-technical way" | No jargon intimidation |

---

## Example Exchange

**You:** I want you to interview me one question at a time about adding a partner invite feature to my pregnancy tracking app. Give me an example of a response to make me think and explain the technical terms in a non-technical way.

**AI:** Great! Let's figure out exactly what you need. First question:

**How will users send an invite to their partner?**

For example, you might say: "They tap a button and enter their partner's email address" or "They share a special link via text message."

This helps me understand the user flow—that's just the step-by-step journey someone takes to complete an action in your app.

**You:** They enter an email and we send an invite link.

**AI:** Perfect! Next question:

**What happens when the partner clicks that invite link?**

For example: "They create their own account" or "They get instant access without signing up" or "They see a preview and then decide to join."

This is about the onboarding experience—how new users first interact with your app...
