## Key Teaching Concept

**JD's Interview Prompt:**
> "I want you to interview me one question at a time. Give me an example of a response to make me think and explain the technical terms in a non-technical way."

**Why this matters:**
- Founders don't know what to ask
- This flips the dynamic - AI asks THEM
- Reduces intimidation
- Teaches terminology naturally
- Creates specification document
